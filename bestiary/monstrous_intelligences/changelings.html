Changelings

Changelings are a broad term for creatures that find means to blend into society within the Kingdoms. The nature of these beings and the manner in which they pass for other species are only vaguely understood - relying on the most infamous and obvious of their kind for clues that they even exist. Rumours and legends are abound of these unseen enemies - innumerable folk cures seek to protect their infants and loved ones from being replaced or ‘taken’ and it is a blessing that perhaps the true nature of this threat is largely ungrasped by all but a few. It is a blessing too that, for the most part, these creatures do not seek dominance or nefarious ends. That just leaves the all the more pressing question of - what do they seek?
