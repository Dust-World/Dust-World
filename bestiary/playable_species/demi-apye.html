Demi-Apye

Apye live among humanity in most places and breed true with their neighbours. Humans are often drawn to the apye, mistaking the absence of doubt for an intoxicating confidence or being entranced by their free, unconnected attitudes - apye themselves tend to be drawn to the unpredictability of humans and endlessly fascinated by their doubts and dreams. These relationships do not always work out - with both sides of the joining being equally likely to find the subtle difference in perspectives spiralling away and pulling them apart. But human-apye families are not uncommon, though their acceptance can vary throughout the Kingdoms.

Offspring of such unions inherit the empathy of their apye parent and the presence of doubt from their human side. They tend towards the tall, but are not necessarily slender and many can sustain fat deposits and develop bulk through physical labour - which can often disagree with apye diets!
