Gazetteer

The World of Dust is one of seemingly great antiquity: everywhere in the East can be found ruins and edifices that speak of the majesty of powers long faded - be they vestiges of the Old Empire or of peoples even older. The lands these relics can be found in seem similarly aged - though, unlike the artifice of thinking people, the old age of the natural world is a moribund one: the seas have spoiled and expanses of the land slowly turn to sand, or mire into uninhabitable swamps; Such are the scars of past calamities - constant reminders to those peoples who struggle to survive in the present that their future is anything but certain.
