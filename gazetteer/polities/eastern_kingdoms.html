The Eastern Kingdoms

The Kingdoms in the east are the descendants of the once great Empire that came before. Stretching all along the blunted sickle of arable land beginning with the sunken forest in the north down to the sodden, saline delta of the south, the Empire stretched across the Devil’s Spine Mountains multiple time in its history and across the sea - where some say its true might lay. Many, many generations ago, with the corruption of the sea, this Empire was sundered and the land fell into years of chaos. The Kingdoms are all that remain of this heritage - pale imitations almost turned feral by the limited space and resources that each jealously guards from all of its fellows. The Kingdoms vary in quality, size and type from oppressive theocracies to magiocratic federations but are almost all ruled by humans - the lore and art of grasping at scraps being that race’s greatest legacy.
