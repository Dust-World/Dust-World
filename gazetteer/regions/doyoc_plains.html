The Doyoc Plains

North-west of the Kremlands, past the protective arc of the Sentinels the rich, dark soil grows thin and dusty, the arable plains receding into an arid, windy plain. Across this rocky landscape were once dotted ancient cities from the Old Empire - withered and waning since the souring of the sea, reduced to little more than the seats of warlords and bandit kings who draped themselves in the abandoned fineries of the past. These city states made their wealth in slaves and cattle - subjugating the nomadic tribes that eked out a life on the plains, raiding border states on the coast, or terrorizing the foothills of the Sentinels.
