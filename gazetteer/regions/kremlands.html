The Kremlands

The Kremlands constitute the most pristine, well-developed land in all of the Kingdoms - being far enough from the Devilâ€™s Spine Mountains for dust and groth to be all but unheard of, but insulated from the Blighted Coast by hills and low peaks - known locally as the Sentinels. A land of rivers, forests and farmlands, the Kremlands are by no means a bucolic idyll - its numerous petty states wage near constant war over every inch of rich black soil. Those with a skill at arms are in constant work here at the heart of the Kingdoms, bulking out the ever-warring Dukes, Kings, Lords and various other stylings of tyrant. First among the nations of the Kremlands are the Dutchies of Kremyat, whose ancient walled cities squat at key points amidst the network of rivers and canals, making them rich from taxes.
