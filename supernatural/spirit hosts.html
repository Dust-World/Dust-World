Spirit Hosts

Though Gods and their creeds can be found wherever there are sophonts to plead to them, they are universally silent - moving, their adherents insist, in a way that defies mortal understanding: influencing their followers spiritually, manipulating fate and bringing about miracles. ‘Divine’ magic however does not seem to have its origins in some external plane or hall of deities, instead being the manifestation of any group of passionate, motivated individuals who can claim a common purpose or history. Fuelled by faith and common cause, the spirits of those who die with such a group’s convictions earnestly held are known to leave a spark of their motive force which joins with all such motes which came before, forming what are known as ‘Spirit Hosts’ - a web of belief, charged through faith, that connects all that pay homage to it - regardless of distance. Those who call upon divine magic channel this energy into wondrous effects. While many hosts arise from the worship of gods or other pagan beliefs, potent hosts have also arisen from orders of warriors, the unity of particular communities and other bands whose beliefs and passions find root in many people.
